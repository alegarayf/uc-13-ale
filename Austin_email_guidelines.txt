Overall Goal: Build an agent that can rapidly triage a data room, surface diligence questions, identify risk areas, and help our deal team form an initial underwriting view before we go deep with advisors.
 
1. Business model overview
	•	Help us quickly understand what the company does, how it makes money, who it serves, and what drives growth.  Items to analyze:
	◦	Revenue model: project-based, recurring, usage-based, retainers, licenses, marketplace, services, etc.
	◦	Key products / services and margin profile by offering
	◦	Customer segments, verticals, and end markets
	◦	Sales motion: founder-led, enterprise sales, channel-led, inbound, partner-driven
	◦	Revenue visibility: backlog, contracted revenue, pipeline, renewal cadence, retainers, MSAs/SOWs
	◦	Dependency on any key vendor, platform, channel, or partner
	◦	What has changed recently in the business model, pricing, GTM, or customer mix
2. Customer quality and concentration
	•	Assess whether revenue is durable, diversified, expanding, or at risk.  Items to analyze:
	◦	Customer concentration by revenue and gross profit
	◦	Top customer history over time: growth, churn, margin, payment behavior
	◦	Cohort performance by customer vintage
	◦	Net revenue retention / gross retention, if applicable
	◦	Logo churn vs. revenue churn
	◦	Customer tenure and renewal patterns
	◦	Customer health indicators: utilization, complaints, discounts, late payments, declining spend
	◦	Contract terms: termination rights, renewal mechanics, pricing escalators, exclusivity, change-of-control provisions
	◦	Exposure to one-time projects versus embedded / recurring relationships
3. Financial trend analysis
	•	Identify whether the financial story is improving, deteriorating, or being distorted by mix, timing, addbacks, or accounting.  Items to analyze:
o    Revenue growth by month / quarter / year
o    Gross margin expansion or compression
o    EBITDA margin trends
o    Contribution margin by customer, product, vertical, office, geography, or employee group
o    Revenue bridge: price, volume, mix, new logos, churn, expansion
o    Cost structure: fixed vs. variable, operating leverage, hiring ahead of revenue
o    Addback quality and recurrence
o    Seasonality and timing effects
o    Working capital trends  
o    Cash conversion and EBITDA-to-cash flow bridge
o    Budget vs. actual performance
o    Forecast achievability versus historical run-rate
4. KPI and operating metric assessment
	•	Extract and assess the KPIs that actually matter for the business.
	•	For services / consulting businesses:
o    Bill rates by role, customer, vertical, and project type
o    Utilization by employee, role, team, and geography
o    Bench size and bench cost
o    Gross margin by project / client / delivery team
o    Contractor vs. employee mix
o    Revenue per FTE
o    Bookings, backlog, pipeline conversion, average sales cycle
o    Delivery capacity versus sales pipeline
o    Attrition / turnover
	•	For healthcare / regulated services:
o    Census / client count trends
o    Payor mix
o    Caregiver / clinician capacity and utilization
o    Compliance incidents
o    Credentialing / licensure status
o    Referral sources
o    Wage pressure and labor availability
o    Revenue per client / visit / hour
o    Denials, collections, and aging trends
5. Contract and legal risk review
	•	Surface terms that could matter economically or create closing / post-close risk.  Items to analyze:
o    Customer contracts with termination for convenience
o    Change-of-control consent requirements
o    Exclusivity, MFN, non-compete, non-solicit, or restrictive covenants
o    Vendor agreements with pricing resets or cancellation rights
o    Platform / reseller / channel dependencies
o    Employee, contractor, and commission agreements
o    Founder / key employee agreements
o    Litigation, disputes, threatened claims
o    Data privacy / security obligations
o    IP ownership, assignment, open-source exposure
o    Insurance gaps or unusual indemnities
6. Quality of revenue and earnings flags
	•	Help us identify issues before QofE or legal diligence formally kicks off.  Items to analyze:
o    Revenue recognized before cash collection or delivery
o    One-time revenue included in run-rate
o    Project revenue booked near period-end
o    Unusual credits, rebates, discounts, refunds, or concessions
o    Topline growth driven by low-margin work
o    Margin expansion driven by underinvestment
o    Addbacks that are really recurring costs
o    Owner expenses that may not fully go away
o    Customer-level profitability outliers
o    Changes in accounting policy or revenue recognition
o    Unusual AR aging, write-offs, or collections issues
7. Forecast and underwriting support
	•	Help turn data room contents into a first-pass underwriting view.  Items to analyze:
o    Whether forecast assumptions are supported by historical performance
o    Revenue build by customer / cohort / pipeline / capacity
o    Sales pipeline quality and conversion assumptions
o    Hiring plan feasibility
o    Margin improvement assumptions
o    Required investment to achieve plan
o    Downside case sensitivities
o    Key value creation levers
o    What assumptions require management validation
o    What should be diligence priorities before LOI / before close
8. Red flag and diligence question generation
	•	Produce a prioritized list of questions for management, bankers, and advisors.  Example outputs:
o    Top 10 diligence issues
o    Top 10 questions for management or the advisor
o    Missing data request list
o    “Things that do not reconcile” list
o    “Claims in CIM not supported by data room” list
9. Deal team workflow and output format
	•	Design the agent to create usable work product, not just generic summaries.  Potential outputs:
o    One-page company overview
o    Deal risks and mitigants grid
o    Diligence tracker
o    Data room gap list
o    Customer concentration summary
o    Financial trend summary
o    KPI dashboard
o    Management question list
o    Source-linked analysis with citations to specific documents / tabs / pages
o    Confidence scoring: high / medium / low support for each conclusion
